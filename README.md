# Missile Defense

Missile Defense is a 2D arcade-style defense game where the player must protect their buildings from waves of incoming enemy attacks. The player launches defensive missiles and times their explosions to destroy threats before they reach the ground. Each wave becomes more intense, requiring quick reactions, careful timing, and smart use of resources.

The game is built around a simple but challenging gameplay loop: survive enemy waves, defeat powerful bosses, earn resources, and use those resources to improve future runs. As the player progresses, upgrades make it possible to last longer, defend more effectively, and take on tougher challenges.

Instead of focusing on exploration, Missile Defense is centered on fast-paced defense gameplay. The goal is to hold the line against increasingly dangerous attacks, adapt to new threats, and overcome unique boss fights that push the player’s strategy and timing.

## Gameplay Overview

- Defend your buildings from incoming enemy missiles
- Launch defensive missiles and use their explosions to destroy threats
- Survive increasingly difficult waves
- Face boss encounters that introduce new mechanics
- Earn resources during each run
- Spend resources on upgrades to improve future attempts

## Features

- 2D arcade-inspired defense gameplay
- Wave-based enemy progression
- Boss fights with unique attack patterns
- Upgrade system that rewards repeated play
- Simple, focused gameplay built around timing and strategy

## Current Project Goal

This project is focused on creating a modern take on classic missile defense gameplay by combining wave survival, boss fights, and upgrade-based progression. The goal is to keep the core gameplay easy to understand while adding enough variety and progression to make repeated runs rewarding.
